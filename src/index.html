Game Intructions

  1. First, press the PLAY button to start playing.
  2. Select the cards to reveal the hidden superheroes.
  3. You should open two cards at the same time.
  4. If the two superheroes match, both cards will remain open.
  5. When all superheroes show up with their peers, you will have won!
